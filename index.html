Business Model

Last Updated, Aug 25, 2025

Don’t expect a typical webstore with add-to-cart convenience. At Narwhal-Industries, we craft our products in small runs of 6 to 50 units or custom-built for individuals, emphasizing quality over quantity. Most products are sold in person at Full Custom Music Repair. We don’t list on eBay, Reverb, or other online platforms, as we believe small, independent music stores offer the best experience for our customers. For direct purchases, please check with our 3rd party dealers first. We also offer consulting, engineering, and product testing services.

Cardigan Tube Preamp

Last Updated, Aug 25, 2025

The Cardigan is our platform for compact tube preamps, sized like our other pedals. It runs on a center-negative tip 9–12V power supply (minimum 400mA), with an internal DC-to-DC converter generating 100V for the audio circuit, delivering rich, high-quality tone that will keep you warm all winter.

Snorkeler

Last Updated, Aug 25, 2025

Out of development and coming soon. The Snorkeler is a JFET Overdrive, deconstructed from an amp of similar name. We've got that 1990s grind.

Taco

Last Updated, Aug 25, 2025

The Taco, now in stock, is an overdrive-boost pedal inspired by our Jose-style modifications and the Gordon 2-in-1 prototype amp, delivering versatile, dynamic tones for guitarists.

Gordon

Last Updated, Aug 25, 2025

Did the Jose mods finially make it to the east coast? The Gordon, currently in development, is our prototype amp and template for the Jose 3-in-1 and Steath 2-in-1 mods. A Snorkeler version is coming soon.

About

Since 2010, musicians have enjoyed the exceptional sound of Narwhal-Industries. To consistently deliver our great tone, we carefully select only high-quality electronic components and original designs to bring you unprecedented quality, full-bodied tone and feel. Our boutique lineup includes amplifiers, fuzz, overdrive, and boost pedals, handbuilt in Lemoyne, Pennsylvania. Reward yourself today with the quality, tone, and feel of Narwhal-Industries. As a musician-owned business, we invite you to experience the unique quality of Narwhal-Industries.
